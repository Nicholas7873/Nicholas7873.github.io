About the UCI Rocket Project Solids Team

The UCI Rocket Project Solids team is a subteam of the UCI Rockect Project whoese goal is to design, build, and fly high powered solid rockets to the highest achievable altitude. Working in teams of 5 members collaborate on the design of their own individual rockets. This allows for enhanced creativity during the design and assembly process.

Design Tools

The UCI Rocket Project primarily makes use of the design program Open Rocket in order to design optimized high powered solid rockets. Open Rocket has proven to be not only be a useful design tool but also a simulator. Rockets designed on Open Rocket can be run through simulations on the same software with descent accuracy allowing for the streamlining of the design process. In addition, Solidworks is used to model parts and run flow simulations in conjunction with Open Rocket to aid in the design process
