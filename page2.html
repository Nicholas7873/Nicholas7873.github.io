Resonant Orbit Calculator

Purpose of the Calculator

Originally the main purpose of the resonant orbit calculator was to provide an easy method to set up a communications relay network of arbitrary size such that the individual satellites would be evenly dispersed in their final orbits. In addition the calulator would provide the neceessary energy changes required to set up such a network. Ultimately the calculator proved be a success especially when using it in tandem with the game Kerbal Space Program. In addition, this calculator could also be used to setup simple communications systems around the earth. However, the calulator is limited by its simplicity

How the Orbit Calculator Works

The orbit calculator makes use of both Kepler's laws and Newton's universal law of gravitation in order to perfrom the neceessary calculations. In order to move the satellites between orbits a simple Hohmann transfer is utilised. The first step to placing the satellites into their proper orbits is to calulate the energy required to get the satellites from their initial altitude to the target altitude. Then, if a resonance orbit is required calulations will be done to determine the energy required to achieve the resonance orbit once the satellites reach their targted altitude, or apoapsis. Finally, the energy needed to circularize the orbit is calculated. Once the calculations are complete for all the required manuevers the results are displayed to the user.
